About this service

Electrical faults are frustrating because the symptom is rarely where the problem is. A tripping safety switch could be a failed appliance, water in an outdoor point, or a damaged cable in the roof.

We diagnose faults methodically with the right test gear, find the actual cause, and quote the repair before doing the work, so you're not paying for guesswork.

What's included

  • Set diagnostic call-out to find the fault
  • Systematic testing with proper instruments
  • Clear explanation of what's wrong
  • Fixed quote for the repair before work proceeds
  • Safe isolation of dangerous faults
  • Compliance certificate for notifiable repairs

Fault finding & repairs FAQs

Why does my safety switch keep tripping?

Usually a faulty appliance, moisture in a circuit, or a damaged cable. We test to find which, then fix it. Don't keep resetting it, that's a sign something's wrong.

Do you charge a call-out for fault finding?

Yes, fault finding has a set diagnostic call-out to cover finding the cause. We then quote any repair before proceeding.

Half my power points are dead, can you help?

Yes, that's a classic fault-finding job, often a tripped circuit or a single failed point breaking the circuit. We'll track it down.

Is a hot or buzzing switch dangerous?

It can be, stop using it and book a diagnostic visit. If it's smoking or burning, switch off the power if safe and call 000.
