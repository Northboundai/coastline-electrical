Electrical renovations

Renovating? Get the wiring right while the walls are open. Rewires, new circuits, kitchens, bathrooms and more.

Book this service (02) 4321 1234

About this service

A renovation is the ideal time to sort the electrical, while the walls are open and access is easy. Getting it right now is far cheaper than retrofitting once everything's lined and painted.

We handle renovation wiring of all sizes, from a single room to a full rewire, and work to your builder's program so the electrical never holds up the job.

What's included

  • Site inspection and electrical plan
  • Rough-in wiring at frame stage
  • New circuits, points, data and lighting
  • Switchboard upgrade if the load requires it
  • Fit-off, testing and commissioning
  • Compliance certificate (CCEW) on completion

Electrical renovations FAQs

Do you work with my builder?

Yes. We coordinate with your builder and trades and work to the program so the electrical stays on schedule.

Can you do a partial rewire?

Yes, we do anything from a single room to a full rewire, depending on what your renovation needs and the condition of existing wiring.

Do I need a switchboard upgrade for a reno?

Often a renovation adds enough load to warrant it. We'll assess and include it in the quote if needed.

Can you add data, TV and extra points?

Yes, renovation is the perfect time to add data, TV points and extra power exactly where you want them.
