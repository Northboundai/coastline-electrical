EV charger installation

Charge at home the easy way. We install EV chargers safely, matched to your supply, with the load management they need.

Book this service (02) 4321 1234

About this service

A home EV charger is far more convenient than relying on public chargers, but it needs to be installed by a licensed electrician who understands load, supply and safety, not just bolted to the wall.

We assess your switchboard and supply, recommend the right charger for your car and home, and install it on a dedicated circuit with proper protection and load management so it charges reliably without tripping anything.

What's included

  • Supply and switchboard load assessment
  • Advice on the right charger for your car and home
  • Dedicated, protected charging circuit
  • Charger mounting and commissioning
  • Load management setup where required
  • Compliance certificate (CCEW) on completion
Book this service

EV charger jobs are quoted after a site inspection because supply, switchboard and cable run all affect the price. The written quote is fixed.

Why book it with us

Matched to your home

We check your supply and switchboard capacity before recommending a charger, so you don't end up with one your home can't properly run.

Safe, dedicated circuit

Your charger goes on its own protected circuit with the right safety devices, not piggy-backed off an existing one.

Smart load management

Where needed, we set up load management so the charger backs off when the house is drawing heavily, avoiding nuisance trips.

Solar-friendly

Got solar? We can set things up so you charge off your own power where possible, getting more value from your panels.

EV charger installation FAQs

Which EV chargers do you install?

We install reputable single- and three-phase chargers and can recommend one to suit your car and home, or fit a charger you've already bought if it's compliant.

Do I need to upgrade my switchboard for an EV charger?

Sometimes. Older boards may need work to add a safe dedicated circuit. We'll tell you at the inspection so the quote is accurate.

Can I charge from my solar?

In many cases yes. Some chargers support solar-aware charging, and we can advise what's possible with your setup.

How long does installation take?

Most home installs are completed in a few hours to half a day, depending on the cable run and any switchboard work.
