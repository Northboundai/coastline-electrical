Ceiling fan installation

Stay comfortable through the Coast summer. We supply and install ceiling fans, or fit your own, balanced and quiet.

Book this service (02) 4321 1234

About this service

Ceiling fans are one of the cheapest ways to stay comfortable through a Central Coast summer, using a fraction of the power of air-conditioning and keeping the air moving year-round.

We install ceiling fans to new or existing points, supply quality fans if you need them, and make sure they're properly balanced so there's no wobble or rattle.

What's included

  • Safe removal of old fans or light fittings
  • Supply of quality fans if needed
  • Secure mounting to a fan-rated fixing
  • Wiring to a new or existing point
  • Balancing and testing
  • Tidy clean-up

Ceiling fan installation FAQs

Can you install a fan where there's only a light?

Usually yes, but the fixing point must be rated to hold a fan safely. We'll check and let you know if any extra work is needed.

Do you supply the fans?

We can supply quality fans to suit your room, or install a fan you've bought yourself.

Can you add a remote or wall control?

Yes, we can install remote-controlled fans or add wall controls during the install.

My fan wobbles, can you fix it?

Yes. Wobble is usually a balance or mounting issue, both of which we can sort.
