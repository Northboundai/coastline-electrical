Rental property electrical

About this service

Keeping a rental property electrically safe and compliant is a legal responsibility, and a hassle if your sparky is unreliable. We make it easy for Central Coast landlords and managing agents.

From smoke alarm compliance and safety switch checks to repairs and certificates, we schedule reliably, deal with tenants courteously, and get the documentation back to you promptly.

What's included

  • Smoke alarm compliance checks and installation
  • Safety switch (RCD) checks and installation
  • General electrical repairs and maintenance
  • Tenant access coordination
  • Compliance certificates and reports
  • Reliable, scheduled service

Rental property electrical FAQs

Do you work with managing agents?

Yes. Agents regularly hand us multiple properties; we coordinate access, do the work and report back with certificates.

What are the rental smoke alarm rules in NSW?

NSW has specific smoke alarm requirements for rentals. We make sure your property complies and provide the documentation.

Can you coordinate access with tenants?

Yes, we arrange a suitable time directly with tenants and keep it courteous and low-hassle.

Do you provide compliance certificates?

Yes, we supply the relevant compliance documentation and certificates for your records and your insurer.
