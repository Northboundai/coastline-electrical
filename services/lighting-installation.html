Lighting installation

Brighter rooms, lower bills, no flicker. From LED downlights to outdoor and security lighting, done cleanly.

Book this service (02) 4321 1234

About this service

Good lighting transforms how a home feels and how much you pay to run it. Swapping old halogens for modern LEDs is one of the most cost-effective upgrades you can make, and it's a job we do every week across the Coast.

Whether it's a full downlight refresh, feature pendants over the kitchen island, or outdoor and security lighting, we install it neatly and make sure it's efficient and easy to live with.

What's included

  • Removal of old or failed fittings
  • Supply and install of LED downlights, pendants or outdoor lights
  • Dimmer and sensor options
  • Matched drivers to avoid flicker and buzz
  • Safe, tidy install with minimal mess
  • Compliance certificate where applicable

Why book it with us

Lower power bills

LEDs use a fraction of the power of halogens and last far longer, so you save on both energy and replacement globes.

Brighter, better light

Modern LEDs give a cleaner, more even light, with dimmable and warm-white options to suit each room.

No flicker, no buzz

Properly matched drivers and dimmers mean no flickering or buzzing, which is the usual sign of a cheap or mismatched install.

Indoor and out

Downlights, pendants, strip lighting, outdoor, deck, garden and security lighting, all installed to the same tidy standard.

Lighting installation FAQs

Can you replace my old halogen downlights with LEDs?

Yes, it's one of our most common jobs. We remove the old fittings and install efficient, dimmable LEDs that run cooler and cost far less.

Can I keep my existing switches and dimmers?

Sometimes, but old dimmers often aren't LED-compatible and cause flicker. We'll let you know if a switch or dimmer needs swapping.

Do you do outdoor and security lighting?

Yes, including sensor lights, flood lights, deck and garden lighting, all weather-rated and safely installed.

Can you quote from photos?

For straightforward lighting jobs, often yes. Send photos when you book and we'll give you an upfront price.
