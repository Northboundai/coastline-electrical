About this service

Downtime costs your business money, so commercial electrical work has to be planned, compliant and done around the way you operate. We look after Central Coast shops, offices, cafes and small business.

From fit-out lighting and power to switchboard work, exit and emergency lighting and ongoing maintenance, we keep your premises safe, compliant and running, scheduling the disruptive parts outside your busy times.

What's included

  • Site inspection and scope
  • Fit-out lighting, power and data
  • Switchboard and distribution work
  • Exit and emergency lighting installation and testing
  • Test-and-tag and maintenance
  • Compliance documentation

Commercial electrical FAQs

Can you work outside our trading hours?

We schedule disruptive work around your trading and operating hours wherever possible, within business-hours availability. We don't run a 24/7 service.

Do you do exit and emergency lighting?

Yes, installation, testing and certification of exit and emergency lighting to keep your premises compliant.

Can you handle our fit-out?

Yes, we do fit-out lighting, power, data and switchboard work, coordinated with your builder and trades.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we can set up regular test-and-tag, lighting maintenance and electrical checks for your premises.
