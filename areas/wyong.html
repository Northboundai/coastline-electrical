Electrician in Wyong

Town homes, rural-residential blocks and businesses around Wyong, looked after by licensed local electricians. Quoted upfront.

Book Online (02) 4321 1234

Wyong is an older town centre surrounded by everything from established homes to rural-residential blocks and acreage out toward the hinterland. That range means we see a bit of everything here, from standard suburban jobs to shed power, pumps and supply work on bigger blocks. Coastline Electrical does licensed, scheduled electrical work across Wyong and the surrounding areas, quoted upfront and done properly.

Established homes and ageing switchboards

Many Wyong homes have been around long enough that their switchboards and wiring are due for attention. Upgrading to a modern board with RCD safety switches is one of the most worthwhile jobs you can do, both for safety and to handle the loads a modern household runs. We also find and fix the faults that older wiring tends to throw up over time.

Sheds, pumps and rural-residential power

Out on the larger Wyong blocks, electrical needs go beyond the house, sub-mains to sheds and granny flats, power for pumps and tanks, outdoor and security lighting across a property, and safe outdoor points and isolation. We're comfortable with this kind of work and make sure everything is properly protected and compliant, which matters even more when circuits run long distances outdoors.

Everyday home electrical

We do all the regular jobs Wyong homes need: extra power points, LED lighting upgrades, ceiling fans, exhaust fans, smoke alarm installs and safety switch upgrades. If you've got a backlog of small electrical jobs, it's usually most cost-effective to book them in together.

Local businesses and sheds

Wyong's businesses, from the town centre shops to light-industrial and trade premises, call us for lighting, power, switchboard work and maintenance. We schedule around your operating hours and keep the work certified and compliant.

Booked in, not 24/7

We don't run an around-the-clock emergency service in Wyong. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, so you get a licensed electrician who shows up on time with an upfront quote. If something's dangerous, call 000, and for a loss of power call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work, book online or call and we'll find a time that suits.

Wyong electrician FAQs

Do you do shed and acreage power around Wyong?

Can you upgrade an old switchboard on a Wyong home?

Yes, switchboard upgrades are one of our most common jobs. We quote upfront after a quick look and issue a compliance certificate on completion.
