Gosford is the heart of the Central Coast, and the electrical work it needs is just as varied as the suburb itself. From the older brick-and-tile homes up around the hospital and East Gosford, to the new apartment towers along the waterfront and the shops and offices through the CBD, we work across the lot. Coastline Electrical is a local team, so when you book a Gosford job you get a sparky who actually knows the area, not a contractor driving up the M1 from Sydney.

The electrical jobs we do most in Gosford

A lot of Gosford's housing stock is 30 to 50 years old, which means switchboards are the number-one job we get called for. Plenty of homes around Gosford still run on old ceramic fuses with no safety switches, and upgrading them to a modern board with RCD protection is one of the best safety improvements you can make. We also do a steady run of lighting upgrades, extra power points, ceiling fans, smoke alarm installs and fault finding when circuits start tripping for no obvious reason.

EV chargers and apartment living

With more EVs on Gosford driveways and in apartment car parks every month, home charger installs have taken off. For houses it's usually straightforward, but the waterfront units bring their own challenges around metering, common property and load. We assess each one properly before quoting so you know it will pass and work safely, rather than finding out the hard way after the gear is on the wall.

Business and commercial work in the CBD

Gosford's CBD is full of small businesses, professional offices and retail, and we handle fit-out lighting, power, switchboard work, exit and emergency lighting checks, and general maintenance for them. We schedule commercial jobs around your trading hours wherever we can, so we're not pulling power in the middle of your busiest part of the day.

Booked in, not a 2am callout

Here's the honest bit: we don't run a 24/7 emergency service in Gosford or anywhere else. What we do is scheduled work and urgent priority bookings during business hours, which means a real electrician who turns up when we say, with the right parts, and quotes you upfront. If something is genuinely dangerous, sparking, burning or smoking, call 000, and for a loss of power across the street call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For everything else, we'll get you booked in fast.

Why Gosford locals call us back

Most of our Gosford work comes from repeat customers and referrals, and that's the way we like it. You'll get a fixed quote before we start, tidy work that's left clean, and a compliance certificate for your records. Whether it's a single power point in East Gosford or a full switchboard and rewire near the waterfront, you can book online in a couple of minutes or give us a call.

Gosford electrician FAQs

How quickly can you get to a job in Gosford?

We hold same-week appointments where availability allows, plus urgent priority slots during business hours. Book online and we'll confirm the soonest time that suits.

Do you work on the Gosford waterfront apartments?

Yes. We do unit and apartment electrical work, including EV charger feasibility, but strata and common-property jobs sometimes need building manager or owners' corporation approval first, which we'll talk you through.

Do you charge a call-out fee in Gosford?

For fault finding we charge a set diagnostic call-out, then quote the repair before doing the work. For standard jobs you get an upfront price, no separate call-out surprise.
