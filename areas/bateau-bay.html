Electrician in Bateau Bay

Family homes and businesses around Bateau Bay, looked after by licensed local electricians. Booked in, quoted upfront.

Bateau Bay is a settled, family-friendly suburb close to Shelly Beach and the Bay Village centre, with a good stock of established brick homes and a strong local community. The electrical work here is classic suburban, switchboard upgrades, lighting, fans, extra points and the occasional fault to chase down, with a steady flow of EV chargers as more households make the switch. Coastline Electrical does licensed, scheduled work right across Bateau Bay.

Established homes, modern needs

Many Bateau Bay homes were built decades ago and are now running loads their original switchboards never anticipated. Upgrading to a safe, modern board with RCD safety switches is one of the most common and worthwhile jobs we do here, and it sets the home up for air-conditioning, an EV charger or a future renovation.

LED lighting and comfort

We do a lot of lighting work in Bateau Bay, replacing old halogen downlights with efficient LEDs, adding pendants and outdoor lighting, and fitting sensor lights for driveways and entries. Ceiling fans are popular too, keeping homes comfortable through summer without leaning on air-conditioning the whole time.

EV chargers for the family driveway

As more Bateau Bay families go electric, home charger installs have become a regular job. We assess your switchboard and supply, recommend the right charger and install it safely on its own circuit, so it charges reliably overnight without tripping anything.

Everyday jobs and fault finding

Extra power points, exhaust fans, smoke alarms, safety switch upgrades and general fault finding round out the work we do for Bateau Bay homes. When something keeps tripping or a circuit goes dead, we find the real cause and fix it properly rather than guessing.

Scheduled work, not 24/7

We don't offer 24/7 emergency callouts in Bateau Bay. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, with licensed electricians and upfront quotes. If something is dangerous, call 000, and for a power outage call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work, book online or give us a call and we'll sort a time that suits.
