Electrician in Woy Woy

The Woy Woy Peninsula has a character all its own, older fibro and brick homes, a strong rental market, and plenty of long-term locals who've been here for decades. The electrical work reflects that: a lot of ageing switchboards, wiring that's seen better days, and rental properties that need to be kept safe and compliant. Coastline Electrical knows the Peninsula well and does honest, scheduled work for Woy Woy, Blackwall, Booker Bay and Ettalong.

Older homes mean older wiring

Many Woy Woy homes still have their original switchboards and, in some cases, sections of old wiring that predate modern safety standards. We see ceramic fuses, no safety switches, and the odd bit of cloth-insulated or aluminium wiring. Upgrading the switchboard and adding RCD safety switches is the single most important safety step for these homes, and we do a lot of it across the Peninsula.

Rental compliance for landlords and agents

Woy Woy has a big rental market, and we work with local landlords and managing agents on smoke alarm compliance, safety switch checks and the electrical side of keeping a property tenantable. We schedule reliably, deal with tenants courteously, and get the certificates back to you promptly so your paperwork is always in order.

Fault finding done properly

When the power keeps tripping or half the house goes dark, guesswork gets expensive. We track faults down methodically, whether it's a failed appliance, water in an outdoor point, a rodent-chewed cable in the roof, or a tired circuit, and then quote the fix before doing the work. On older Peninsula homes this kind of diagnostic work is some of our most common.

Everyday electrical jobs

Alongside the bigger work, we do all the everyday jobs: extra power points, ceiling fans, LED lighting upgrades, exhaust fans, garden and security lighting, and smoke alarm installs. If you've got a list of small things that have been bugging you, it's often most economical to knock them all over in one booked visit.

No 24/7 callouts, just reliable service

We don't offer round-the-clock emergency callouts on the Peninsula. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, with a licensed electrician and an upfront quote. If something is dangerous, call 000, and for a loss of power call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work in Woy Woy, book online or call and we'll lock in a time that suits.
