Umina Beach sits at the southern tip of the Peninsula, a mix of permanent family homes, holiday houses and a growing number of renovated places as more people move to the area full-time. Coastline Electrical does licensed, scheduled electrical work right across Umina, from a quick power-point job to a full switchboard upgrade and rewire on a place that's being brought back to life.

Renovations are bringing Umina homes up to date

A lot of Umina's older beach houses are being renovated, and the electrical side often needs to catch up with the rest of the work. We handle renovation wiring, new circuits, switchboard upgrades, extra points and data, and kitchen and bathroom fit-out electrical, working to the builder's program so we're not holding the job up. Getting the wiring right during a reno is far cheaper than retrofitting later.

Coastal conditions and outdoor areas

Being a beach suburb, Umina homes cop the salt air and make the most of outdoor living. We install weather-rated outdoor power and lighting, sort corroded fittings, and set up entertaining areas, decks and pools with safe, compliant electrical. Pool and spa wiring in particular has strict safety rules, and we make sure it's done by the book.

Holiday homes and rentals

With plenty of holiday and rental properties around Umina, we do a steady run of smoke alarm compliance, safety switch checks and the kind of tidy, reliable maintenance that keeps a let property safe and presentable. Sensor and timer lighting is popular for places that sit empty between guests.

Switchboards, safety switches and EV chargers

Older Umina switchboards often need upgrading to handle modern loads and to add the RCD safety switches required on today's circuits. We also install home EV chargers, matched to your supply and installed safely, which is increasingly common as more Umina households go electric.

Booked in, not a 24/7 service

We don't run 24/7 emergency callouts in Umina. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, so you get a licensed electrician who turns up when we say with the right gear and an upfront price. If something's dangerous, call 000, and for loss of power call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. Otherwise, book online or give us a call and we'll sort a time.
