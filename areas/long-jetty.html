Electrician in Long Jetty

Renovated cottages, cafes and homes around Long Jetty, looked after by licensed local electricians. Booked in, quoted upfront.

Book Online (02) 4321 1234

Long Jetty has had a real revival, the old beach cottages being renovated, a strip of cafes, bars and shops that draws people from across the Coast, and a community that loves the area's character. The electrical work matches that energy: renovation wiring, hospitality fit-outs, and the steady maintenance that homes and businesses near the lake always need. Coastline Electrical is local and does licensed, scheduled work throughout Long Jetty and the surrounding lakeside suburbs.

Cottage renovations and rewires

So many Long Jetty cottages are being brought up to date, and the wiring usually needs to come along for the ride. We handle renovation wiring, full and partial rewires, switchboard upgrades, new circuits, and the electrical for kitchen and bathroom fit-outs. Old cottages often hide surprises in the walls and roof, so doing the electrical properly during a reno saves grief later.

Hospitality and shop fit-outs

The Long Jetty strip is full of cafes, bars, studios and shops, and we do fit-out electrical for them, lighting design and installation, power for equipment, switchboard work, and exit and emergency lighting to keep them compliant. We schedule the disruptive parts around opening hours so businesses can keep trading.

Lighting that suits the area's style

Long Jetty's renovated homes and venues tend to want lighting with a bit of character, warm dimmable LEDs, feature pendants, and outdoor lighting for decks and gardens that make the most of the lakeside evenings. We install it all cleanly and make sure it's energy-efficient and easy to live with.

Everyday jobs and maintenance

Alongside the bigger work we do the everyday stuff, extra points, fans, smoke alarms, safety switches, and fault finding when something stops working. For the area's rentals and short-stays, smoke alarm and safety compliance is a regular and important job.

Scheduled work, not 24/7 callouts

We don't offer 24/7 emergency callouts in Long Jetty. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, with licensed electricians and upfront quotes. If something is dangerous, call 000, and for a power outage call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work, book online or give us a call.
