Tuggerah has grown fast, with newer housing estates, the Westfield centre, and a busy commercial and business-park precinct off the motorway. The electrical work here leans a little newer than the older Coast suburbs, but new homes have their own needs, and businesses have plenty. Coastline Electrical does licensed, scheduled work across Tuggerah for homeowners and businesses alike.

Newer homes, growing loads

Even relatively new Tuggerah homes end up needing extra power points, additional circuits, ceiling fans, outdoor lighting and EV chargers as families settle in and add to the place. We're often called to add capacity the original build didn't include, and to install the gear that makes a new house actually work for the people living in it.

EV chargers for estate homes

EV charger installs are one of our fastest-growing jobs in Tuggerah, which makes sense given the newer homes and the commute many residents make. We assess your switchboard and supply, recommend a suitable charger, and install it safely on a dedicated circuit with proper load management, so it charges reliably without nuisance tripping.

Lighting and comfort

We do plenty of LED lighting work in Tuggerah, downlights, pendants, outdoor and security lighting, plus ceiling fans to handle the inland summer heat, which runs hotter here than down on the coast. Sensor lighting for driveways and entries is a popular safety and convenience add-on.

Commercial work around Westfield and the business park

Tuggerah's commercial precinct keeps us busy with retail and office fit-outs, warehouse and business-park lighting and power, switchboard work, and exit and emergency lighting compliance. We schedule around trading and operating hours and keep everything certified, so your business stays compliant and running.

Scheduled work, not emergency callouts

We don't offer 24/7 callouts in Tuggerah. Our work is scheduled and urgent priority bookings during business hours, with licensed electricians and upfront quotes. If something is dangerous, call 000, and for loss of supply call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work, book online or call and we'll confirm a time.
