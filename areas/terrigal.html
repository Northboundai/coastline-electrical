Terrigal is one of the Coast's most sought-after addresses, and its homes run the full range from original beach cottages to architect-designed places up on the hill with the big ocean views. That mix keeps our work interesting. Coastline Electrical looks after Terrigal homeowners, holiday-home owners and the cafes and businesses along the esplanade, with licensed work that's booked in, quoted upfront and finished neatly.

Salt air is hard on electrical fittings

If you live near the water in Terrigal, you already know what the salt air does to everything metal. Outdoor power points, garden and pool lighting, switchboards in exposed positions and air-conditioning isolators all corrode faster here than they do inland. A lot of our Terrigal work is replacing rusted, pitted fittings with weather-rated gear that's actually built for a coastal position, so you're not redoing it every couple of years.

Switchboard upgrades on older Terrigal homes

Many of the older Terrigal cottages still have tired switchboards that were never designed for the load a modern home puts through them, dishwashers, induction cooktops, air-con, pool pumps and now EV chargers. We upgrade these to safe, compliant boards with proper RCD safety switches and room to add circuits down the track, which is especially handy if a renovation is on the cards.

Lighting that suits a beach home

Terrigal homes look their best with good lighting, and we install everything from clean LED downlights and feature pendants to outdoor, deck and security lighting that handles the weather. For holiday homes and short-stay rentals, sensor and timer lighting is popular because it keeps the place looking lived-in and safe between guests.

EV chargers for the drive home

Plenty of Terrigal households have made the switch to electric, and a properly installed home charger beats relying on public ones. We assess your switchboard and supply, recommend the right charger, and install it on its own circuit with the safety and load management it needs. For homes with solar, we can set things up so you're charging off your own power where possible.

Scheduled work, not 24/7 callouts

We don't do 2am emergency callouts in Terrigal, and we're upfront about that. We book scheduled and urgent priority work during business hours, which means you get a reliable, licensed electrician rather than a panicked rush job. If anything is dangerous, call 000, and for a power outage call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned work, booking online takes a couple of minutes and we'll confirm a time that suits.

Terrigal electrician FAQs

Do you use coastal-rated fittings in Terrigal?

Where a fitting is exposed to salt air we recommend weather and corrosion-rated gear. It costs a little more upfront but lasts far longer in a beachside position, so it's cheaper over time.

Can you work on Terrigal holiday rentals between bookings?

Yes. We schedule work around your booking calendar where possible, and sensor or timer lighting and smoke alarm compliance are common requests for short-stay properties.

Do you cover the streets up around the Skillion and the hill?

Yes, we cover all of Terrigal including the harder-to-access homes up on the hill. Let us know about parking and access when you book and we'll plan for it.
