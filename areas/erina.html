Erina sits right in the middle of the Coast, home to Erina Fair, a stack of family homes through the surrounding streets, and a thriving strip of businesses along the Central Coast Highway. Coastline Electrical is local, licensed and easy to deal with, with quotes you agree to before we start.

LED lighting upgrades are our most-booked Erina job

A huge amount of our Erina work is lighting. Plenty of homes here were built in the era of halogen downlights, which run hot, fade and chew through power. Swapping them for modern dimmable LEDs makes rooms brighter and cooler and noticeably cuts the lighting portion of your power bill. We also fit pendants over kitchen islands, outdoor and entertaining-area lighting, and sensor lights for driveways and side access.

Keeping Erina families comfortable

Between the western sun and the humid Coast summers, ceiling fans and good ventilation matter. We install and replace ceiling fans, add power points where families actually need them, and sort out the little frustrations like a dead outlet or a light circuit that keeps tripping. Smoke alarm installation and safety switch upgrades are also regular jobs, and both are worth staying on top of.

Commercial and fit-out work near Erina Fair

With so many businesses based around Erina, we do a good amount of commercial work, retail and office lighting, additional power and data, switchboard changes, and exit and emergency lighting. We plan these jobs around trading hours so your business keeps running, and we keep everything compliant and certified.

Switchboards and EV chargers

As Erina's homes take on more load, older switchboards start to struggle. We upgrade them to safe, modern boards with RCD safety switches, which also sets you up nicely if you're adding an EV charger or air-conditioning. EV charger installs are growing fast here, and we make sure each one is matched to your supply and installed safely on its own circuit.

Booked in, done right

We don't run 24/7 callouts. Our work is scheduled and urgent priority bookings during business hours, so you get a licensed electrician who turns up on time with the right gear. Anything dangerous, call 000, and for loss of supply call Ausgrid on 13 13 88. For planned electrical work in Erina, you can book online in a couple of minutes or give us a ring.
