How it works

Three ways we quote a job

Every service above is tagged with how we price it, so you know what to expect before you book.

Standard appointment

Straightforward jobs like power points, fans, lighting and smoke alarms. We give an upfront price and book a time window that suits you.

Diagnostic visit

For faults and intermittent problems. We charge a set call-out to find the cause, then quote the repair before any further work goes ahead.

Site inspection

For bigger jobs like switchboards, EV chargers, renovations and commercial work. We inspect, then send a fixed written quote, no obligation.

Service questions

Can you give a price over the phone?

For standard jobs, often yes, especially if you can send a photo. For switchboards, EV chargers and renovations we prefer a quick site inspection so the written quote is accurate and there are no surprises.

Do you supply the parts and fittings?

Yes. We can supply quality fittings, downlights, fans, chargers and switchboard gear, or install items you've bought yourself. We'll let you know if something you've sourced isn't compliant before we fit it.

Will I get a compliance certificate?

Yes. Notifiable electrical work is issued with a Certificate of Compliance for Electrical Work (CCEW) for your records, your insurer and any future sale.

What if it turns out to be urgent?

We hold urgent priority slots during business hours and will get to you as soon as we can. We don't run a 24/7 emergency service. If it's dangerous, call 000, and for loss of supply call Ausgrid on 13 13 88.

Not sure which service you need?

Tell us what's going on and we'll point you in the right direction. No pressure, no call-out just to talk it through.
