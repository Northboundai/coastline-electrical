# Coastline Electrical Co. — Higgsfield asset prompts

Copy-ready prompts for generating the website's video and image assets. The
site is built to look premium **without** any of these (branded placeholders
and CSS/SVG fallbacks fill every slot), so add them as a quality upgrade and
drop them into the paths below.

**Universal rules for every asset:** premium, modern, trustworthy Australian
trade feel. Natural coastal light. No text, no logos, no readable branding, no
faces clearly visible. Dark navy / electric blue palette with warm light so the
dark overlays keep on-page text readable.

---
